AI BUSINESS IDEA 2026

AI-based Adaptive Learning Systems

My Favourite AI Idea

AI-based adaptive learning systems study students' learning patterns and adjust how they teach based on how students learn.

Application in My World

As a visual leaner, I sometimes struggle to understand concepts when I have to be reading for hours. An AI-based adaptive learning system could detect that I learn better through visuals and provide relevant videos of concepts that I need help with.

Why It Would Be Useful

It personalises the learning experience and makes it easier for usstudents to learn concepts.

Ethical Issues

  • Data Privacy — There needs to be policies in place to ensure that students' learning data is stored securely and not shared without our consent.
  • Overuse/Abuse of the system — It is important for us students to utilise our critical thinking skills and not always rely on the LMS to help us.

My Solutions

  • Data should automatically be protected and students must consent to it being shared with other parties.
  • Students should be encouraged to understand concepts on their own before using the platform.

Example

Khan Academy uses AI to personalise learning paths.
